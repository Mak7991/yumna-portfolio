About Me

I’m Dr. Yumna Ameer — a medical doctor who boldly transitioned into the world of entrepreneurship to solve real problems across real estate, tech, and community development.

After completing my MBBS, I realized my deeper calling was innovation. So, I traded the stethoscope for startups — and have since led large-scale real estate projects, created Pakistan’s first startup marketplace, and mentored Gen-Z builders through real-world learning platforms.

I am currently the CEO of Guru Builders and GenZ Builders & Developers,
and the driving force behind ventures like:

See More

  • Guru Builders & Developers — Delivering smart, affordable housing and commercial solutions tailored for modern investors and communities.

  • Gen-Z Builders & Developers — Revolutionizing real estate with youth-driven concepts, themed projects, and futuristic urban developments.

  • MustStartup — A digital marketplace where users can Buy, Sell, Scale, Tokenize, Learn, Repeat.

  • T20 Towers (Islamabad) — Pakistan’s first cricket-themed luxury development.

  • Al Mirha Mobile Mall & Residency — A visionary hybrid of tech commerce and modern living.

  • School of Entrepreneurs — Early-age entrepreneurial education for youth.
